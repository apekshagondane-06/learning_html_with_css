CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) was first proposed in 1994 by Håkon Wium Lie and Bert Bos as a way to separate the presentation (styling) of web documents from their content (HTML). This separation aimed to improve web development by making it easier to manage and update the look and feel of websites without altering the underlying HTML structure
